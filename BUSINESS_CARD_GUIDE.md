## QR Code Setup

### Step 1: Get Your Website URL
After deploying to Vercel or another platform, you'll have a URL like:
- `https://questiontheday.vercel.app`
- `https://your-custom-domain.com`

### Step 2: Generate QR Code

**Free QR Code Generators:**
1. **QR Code Generator** - https://www.qr-code-generator.com/
   - Free, no signup required
   - Customizable colors
   - Download as PNG or SVG

2. **QR Code Monkey** - https://www.qrcode-monkey.com/
   - Free forever
   - Add logo in center
   - High resolution

3. **Canva** (if you have an account)
   - Professional looking designs
   - Built-in QR code generator

### Step 3: Customize QR Code
For better brand recognition:
- Use your brand colors (pink/purple from the site)
- Add your logo in the center (keep it small)
- Test the QR code before printing!

## Printing Tips

### Print Specs
- **Size**: Standard business card (3.5" x 2")
- **Format**: CMYK color mode
- **Resolution**: 300 DPI minimum
- **Bleed**: 0.125" (ask your printer)

### Recommended Printers
- **Online**: VistaPrint, Moo, GotPrint
- **Local**: FedEx Office, Staples
- **Premium**: Moo (nice thick cards with custom finishes)

### Quantity Suggestions
- Start with 100-250 cards
- Reorder when you have ~20 left
- Update QR code if you change domains!

## What to Say When Handing Out Cards

### Option 1: Before the Interview
> "Thanks for participating! Scan this QR code to watch previous episodes. Your episode will be up in 24 hours!"

### Option 2: After the Interview  
> "Your video will be live tomorrow! Check out the site to see what others have answered. Your episode drops in 24 hours!"

### Option 3: Casual
> "Scan this to see other people's answers. Yours will be on there tomorrow too!"

## Tracking Engagement

### Short URLs (Optional)
Use a URL shortener with analytics:
- **Bit.ly**: Track clicks, see when people scan
- **TinyURL**: Simple and free
- **yourDomain.com/qtd**: Custom short link

Then create a QR code from the short URL to track:
- How many people scan
- When they scan (right away vs later)
- Where they're from

### Analytics Setup
In your website, you can add:
- Google Analytics
- Vercel Analytics (built-in)
- Facebook Pixel (if you have a FB page)

This tells you:
- How many visitors from QR codes
- How long they stay
- Which videos they watch

## Card Design Template

Want a ready-made design? Here's a Canva-style layout:

**Colors to Use:**
- Primary: #ff6b6b (coral pink)
- Secondary: #4ecdc4 (cyan)
- Background: #0a0a0a (black)
- Text: #ededed (off-white)

**Fonts:**
- Header: Bold, modern sans-serif (Inter, Poppins)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (Inter, Open Sans)

## Pro Tips

1. **Test First**: Print 10 cards first, test the QR code
2. **Weatherproof**: Consider glossy finish or plastic cards
3. **Multiple Designs**: Create variations to test which gets more scans
4. **Keep Extras**: Always carry a stack with you
5. **Follow Up**: After interviews, DM participants when their video is live
