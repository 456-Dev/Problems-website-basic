About the project

Each question is asked to roughly 25 people in the same session. All recorded responses are included with minimal editing while meeting a 3 minute time constraint. Each episode is shot, edited and published within the day.

Each person is asked 3 questions: [What would you say is your number one problem right?], [What is your solution?] and [Question of The Day] These first two quesions will be used for a future project {see What Is Your Problem?!}
